About Us

We are a company that manufactures and sells Eco-Friendly shopping bags to businesses that strive to be environmentally responsible. We plan to substitute single-use plastic bags with cloth bags or biodegrable plastic bags to create a strong positive impact on the ecosystem.

Our Mission

As a team, we genuinely believe that ecological consumption and its impact on the world around us is very crucial. Our mission is to decrease single-use bags and instead, have a more durable and eco-friendly product.

Our Story

With the rise of single-use plastic in the world and the amount of plastic that end up in the ocean, which is responsible for the death of millions of aquatic creatures, a change was necessary. OrganiEco had the vision to bring this problem that we have in the 21st century toward an end. The idea of using reusable cloth bags and biodegrable shopping bags would decrease the amount of single-use plastic in the world. We envisioned that this idea would potentially bring the crisis of single-use plastic to an end.
